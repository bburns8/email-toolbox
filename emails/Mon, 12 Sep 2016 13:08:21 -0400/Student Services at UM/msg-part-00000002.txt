We know how important the academic opportunities at University of Montana are, but we also want you to think about what services are available to you at the University of Montana.<br>
<br>
<a href="http://umontana.askadmissions.net/admin/Communications/ClickThru.aspx?qs=oVSG23nwdjIPWDd3BiF4mlKardq58c4rulwrzqwf796tkA6FyeFQFSnLyg8bY9u9">Residence Life</a>: Living in the Residence Halls is a great way to get to know new people and connect with campus on a whole new level. From the Hall Brawl competitions, to floor snacks, to frisbee on the Oval at 1am…living on campus is a once-in-a-lifetime experience!<br>
<br>
<a href="http://umontana.askadmissions.net/admin/Communications/ClickThru.aspx?qs=oVSG23nwdjIPWDd3BiF4mhNQ87b3HdHQYHv6EhFR4V3LRvtIlhKfImFOh3S6gjnz">UM Dining</a>: Our food is awesome. I know, I know, it's our job to tell you that, but we aren't kidding. The award-winning "Food Zoo" offers all-you-care-to-eat meals with an ever-changing choice of entrees, homemade soups, an extensive salad bar, fresh fruits, fresh based desserts, traditional fast food favorites, and a daily vegetarian, vegan and gluten free options. In addition, UM's Farm to College program is acclaimed nationwide, as we bring in over $990,000 worth of locally grown produce right to our kitchen. We know exactly where our delicious food is coming from.<br>
<br>
<a href="http://umontana.askadmissions.net/admin/Communications/ClickThru.aspx?qs=oVSG23nwdjIPWDd3BiF4mofaWJDMrpuAMyQ1gmDoq6AfB9H9WgL6DoLQcx46sTcu">Curry Health Center</a>: We know, you don't choose a college based on the outstanding Health Center. But we also know how important it is for you to be healthy, so you can succeed in school! Plus, your parents will feel better knowing that you've got somewhere to go if you get sick. UM's Curry Health Center has full-time physicians, nurses, counselors, nutrition consultants, dentists, and a pharmacy.<br>
<br>
<a href="http://umontana.askadmissions.net/admin/Communications/ClickThru.aspx?qs=oVSG23nwdjIPWDd3BiF4ms3dMpy4B4HlXiQHSCQy%2btoh4RjDocXwmarV%2fHdV%2fL%2f7">Campus Fitness &amp; Recreation</a>: Staying healthy and fit is easy at UM with our state-of-the-art recreation center, which includes a rock wall, cardio equipment, weights and an indoor running track. You can also enjoy activities courses from billiards to yoga to fly-fishing, to round out your academic class schedule. Part of the fun of coming to University of Montana is our incredible surroundings, so our Campus Rec program also offers an Outdoor Programs Office, where you can rent equipment, take skills courses and go on outdoor adventures with your fellow students.<br>
<br>
<a href="http://umontana.askadmissions.net/admin/Communications/ClickThru.aspx?qs=oVSG23nwdjIPWDd3BiF4mkmA%2b3vwnoFfsOflh9prEonZuJzKT%2fz8IJQs4TkTc8DL">Career &amp; Internship Services</a>: Once you’re here, you may never want to leave, but eventually you will graduate and join the professional world. Our Career Services &amp; Internship Services Offices will help prepare you for employment during and after graduation. From resume review and practice interviews to internships around the world, getting out of classroom experiences will enhance your education and ability to get a job after graduation.<br>
<br>
<a href="http://umontana.askadmissions.net/admin/Communications/ClickThru.aspx?qs=oVSG23nwdjIPWDd3BiF4miYnOKjQMYHp5bXFq%2bxPq4cw6SwOQ2Nv6xzSlY1Lq7un">University Center</a>: The UC is your student union. It is a place for you to study, discover diversity, enjoy entertainment and feel at home away from home. The UC offers a myriad of convenient services including an art gallery, hi-tech study lounge, game room, theater, conferencing services, shipping and mail center, bank and ATM’s, copy center, full-service hair salon, bookstore, market, food court, and Griz Card Center.<br>
<br>
<a href="http://umontana.askadmissions.net/admin/Communications/ClickThru.aspx?qs=oVSG23nwdjIPWDd3BiF4mtTAKsraRN86yLftW9owbRgjrdrHfDnOe70bHaMCU3bL">Disability Services for Students</a>: This office assures that programs of the University of Montana are as accessible to and usable by students with disabilities as they are for any student. Students with disabilities can be assured that you will get reasonable accommodations for your disability. And don’t worry, if you break your right hand snowboarding and you need someone to take notes for you until you heal, Disability Services for Students also serves students with temporary disabilities.
